Frequently Asked Questions

What is NCEXpress?

NCEXpress is a Windows desktop app for CAs to automate Non-Corporate Entity financial statements directly from Tally into polished, print-ready outputs.

Yes. Schedule III (BS/PL/Notes) and Schedule II (PPE movement/useful life) are both covered, with linkages that stay in sync on refresh.

Yes. Internet is required to authenticate the license and run the app. Data processing remains local on your machine.
